Regional areas in NSW have seen a 43% decline in water usage since 2005-06, in response to water efficiency measures for recent droughts. (EPA NSW)

Sydney Water has been convicted and ordered to pay $175,500 by the Land and Environment Court, following the release of almost 3 million litres of untreated sewage into Toongabbie Creek and Parramatta River in October 2018. (EPA, 2021)

To tackle this issue, we have found the optimal locations for our hydro towers in key areas of NSW that are in need of more water. Instead of pumping illegal water wastage into the ocean and promote flooding, we can distribute these water to these areas.
